The history of the MarbleLympics

The history of the marbleLympics is storied and dramatic, filled with unexpected turnarounds, dazzling displays of athletiscism and even some unfourtunate controversy. Bellow is an overview of the games as a whole as well as some particularly polarizing moments from them

MarbleLympics 2016

The 2016 Marble League was the first edition of the Marble League and consisted of 16 teams and 12 events. This season had no Qualification Rounds, and thus no teams were cut.The opening ceremony featured each of the sixteen teams coming out one by one before lining up in a grid. This season featured a different scoring system thatn later seasons as shown bellow.

At the end of the season, it was the Savage Speeders who were victorious, but Mellow Yellow, Thunderbolts, and Team Momo were very close behind.
